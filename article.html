Celebrating Diversity: International Sign Languages Day

International Sign Language Day celebrates the importance of sign languages ​​as part of identity and human rights. Sign languages ​​are more than just hand movements, but rather a complex language system. This celebration encourages us to value linguistic diversity and promote inclusivity for the deaf community. By understanding sign languages, we open up opportunities for more effective communication and greater access for all.
