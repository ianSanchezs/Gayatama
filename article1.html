The Important Role of Sign Language Interpreters in Peparnas 2024, These are the Tasks and Challenges

09 October 2024
Team Power of Duâ€™a

The Ministry of Communication and Informatics (Kominfo) has taken a significant step towards ensuring accessibility for the deaf community at the XVII Solo Peparnas 2024 by providing Sign Language Interpreter (JBI) services at the event's Media Center. This initiative allows deaf individuals to stay informed about the happenings of Peparnas 2024 through press conferences and broadcasts on the Directorate General of IKP Kominfo's Youtube channel.

Ian, one of the 14 JBIs on duty at Peparnas 2024, plays a crucial role in interpreting press conferences held at the Media Center. Based at the TVRI studio and The Royal Surakarta Heritage, Ian's responsibilities extend until the media facilities close at 10 PM each day. As a native son of Solo, Ian feels a sense of pride and fulfillment in contributing to the success of this national event. Prior to his duties at Peparnas XVII, Ian underwent thorough preparation, including researching sports-specific terminology that would be used during press conferences. He expressed gratitude for the opportunity to participate in this national event and return to his hometown. Ian's professional experience includes working as a JBI at the Constitutional Court, Palembang Police, and Palembang TVRI.

Interpreting sign language at the Peparnas XVII Media Center presented unique challenges for Ian, who had to adapt to the camera positioning, lighting, and other settings to ensure clear communication. The Media Center, provided by the Ministry of Communication and Information, serves as a vital platform for journalists to disseminate information and news related to the Peparnas XVII Solo activities, which took place from October 6 to 13, 2024.
