S I B I

SIBI or Sistem Isyarat Bahasa Indonesia is a sign language used by the deaf in Indonesia. SIBI is very important because it allows deaf people to communicate more effectively. SIBI uses hand gestures, facial expressions, and body movements to convey ideas, thoughts, and feelings.
